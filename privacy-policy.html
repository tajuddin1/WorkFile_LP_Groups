LP Solutions Group Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2024

At LP Solutions Group, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services. By accessing the LP Solutions Group website, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

  • 1.1 Personal Information: When you interact with our website or services, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing details, which you provide voluntarily.

  • 1.2 Usage Information: We collect non-personal information about your interactions with our site, including IP addresses, browser type, operating system, device information, pages viewed, and visit timestamps.

  • 1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We utilize cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site usage. Manage your cookie settings through your browser options.

  • 2.1 Personal Information Usage: We use your personal information to deliver requested services, process payments, communicate with you, and for customer support. We may send you promotional materials, which you can opt out of at any time.

  • 2.2 Usage Information: This data helps us improve service delivery, analyze usage trends, and enhance website administration.

  • 3.1 With Service Providers: We share information with third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, under confidentiality agreements.

  • 3.2 For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information when legally required or to protect our rights.

  • We implement security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information but acknowledge that no security system is infallible.

  • We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of any linked third-party sites.

  • Our services do not target anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

  • We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes will be communicated through our website and email notifications.

  • As a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information, including the right to request disclosure of data collection and sharing practices, request deletion of personal information, and opt-out of the sale of personal information.

  • We retain personal information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

  • You have the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. California residents have additional rights as described in Section 8.

  • Information collected may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or any other country where we operate.

  • For any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us at:

    Email: contact@lpgroups.com
